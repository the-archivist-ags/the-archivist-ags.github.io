Garland

Governor of the North

I’m someone who embraces progressive change, I don’t simply nibble around the edges on the policy. Throughout my career, I have worked to design and implement policy that positively impacts the most amount of people as possible, often directly challenging the Washington consensus. I’m going to continue being myself, someone who governs based on collaboration, discourse, and input from others rather than from fear or coercion.
