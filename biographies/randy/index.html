Randy

Former Dixie Speaker Pro Tempore

The following is a lightly edited transcript of Randy's interview with The Archivist.

Drunk: Welcome.

Randy: Thanks.

Drunk: So, shall we begin with a little bit about your personal life? What do you do when not on the clock?

Randy: I sometimes go to the zoo and free the kangaroos. They always find a way back but it’s all part of the job. On occasion, I will go to the funeral home to visit my grandfather. He isn’t dead, we just left him there. I also am very active on the 4chan community “liberal SJWs destroyed by facts and logic and truth”. I would be happy to take any questions if the viewers have some.

Drunk: How has your condition affected your life?

Randy: Which one?

Drunk: The condition in which you believe you are a rat.

Randy: No comment.

Drunk: Do you have any comments on your accident in the House CCE Committee?

Randy: “accident”?

Drunk: Incident.

Randy: Sorry.

Drunk: What got you into politics in the first place?

Randy: No clue really, I just figured “why not get involved in the group that I have hated my entire life and have spent a lot of it campaigning against”?

Drunk: Interesting. I hear you have recently retired from politics to practise law. How is your career there going, and would you ever consider a return to politics?

Randy: Yeah. So law is boring and hard. Consider my retirement a very awesome prank. Although now I know a bit more about the law which is cool.

Drunk: I consider myself pranked. What, if anything, will you be doing when you return to politics? Do you have any positions in mind?

Randy: As you may have known I was going to be the Lieutenant Governor if Korn won. But I was in hibernation at that time. So I couldn’t. I was also considering mayor of DC but JMP would wipe his ass with me. I’m considering possibly senate or house. When I come back, my top priority will be: the abolition of the federal government, the complete banning of catgirls, no exceptions, and state endorsed subway sandwich restaurants. Together as people we must take a stand against catgirls and all their supporters. TOGETHER WE CAN

Drunk: Could you give us a brief rundown of your political career?

Randy: Yes, here is a link to a document explaining it.

Drunk: Thank you. What would you consider your greatest political achievement?

Randy: Either beating teras h or the lotr thing

Drunk: Could you elaborate more on the LOTR scandal? What caused you to get cold feet and expose it?

Randy: I believed it was in the interest of everyone to release it. I had disapproved of the plan for a while and after LOTR expelled me from the party for showing what was really going on, I decided to do it. Hiding it would only cause more damage.

Drunk: I see. What would you consider your greatest challenge or weakness?

Randy: I have no weaknesses.

Drunk: Understandable. Finally, could you sum up your career in one or two sentences?

Randy: Absolute shitfest, swing and a miss. [sic] This is why I am continuing.

Drunk: Interesting. Well, it's been a pleasure Mr. Rhombus. Thank you for your time.
