Our history

We founded this Organization to be a reliable collection of information, to satisfy all our desires to learn. From small beginnings, we hope to rise to become to whom America turns for information.


Our future plans

We hope to codify the lives of our top politicians in their own words through our biographies, and to possibly expand with more staff into analytical articles. Until then, we will continue to release new polls regularly and provide you with a treasure trove of history to help you learn from the past.
