Introducing The Forecast Since our inception, we've run election predictions with a fair bit of success. But us, like all the other pundits, are making these predictions based on a rough guess at who has the more successful campaign, the more skillful debate. The Stage is looking to change that with The Forecast. The Forecast is a data-driven tool that will predict a range of possible outcomes for every election we see in this country. We'll factor in likely voter history, debate skills, campaign performance, and many other components of what makes a candidate succeed. This is a complex tool and we're being cautious before rolling it out for the public to put trust in. The Forecast will be operational by the upcoming federal election, but will be published after so we can quality test and ensure the highest accuracy possible. Anyone interested and skilled in web design is asked to reach out, we're looking to make this a user-interactive tool if possible.
